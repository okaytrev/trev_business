Meet coach Trevar

Trevar is unique in that most coaches have trouble relating to adversity, but he has himself dealt with the many curveballs that life has thrown his way. He grew up in a single family househould, where life felt like it was constantly on the move. He's a recovered addict who has battled with alcochol, tobacco, and video game addictions, along with being a survivor of severe ulcerative colitis. Although he's faced adversity he's overcome it with support and hard work. He is now a well versed technologist, succesful athlete, and a grateful husband and dog dad. Trevar is excited to help others through the journey of life.
